How to read this

A Discourse Atom Topic Model (Arseniev-Koehler & Foster) trains a word-embedding on this corpus alone, then factors it into atoms — recurring patterns of meaning discovered without being told what to look for. Each card is one atom, read through the documents that most embody it (open any in the viewer). The coloured bar shows the mix of literatures it draws on.

For the analogy × tai-chi project: the ★ bridge chip isolates atoms whose core documents come from both the analogy and the embodiment/movement literatures — where analogical structure meets embodied skill. Those documents are your opening reading list.
